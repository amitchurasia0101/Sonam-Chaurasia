About

Hi there! I'm Sonam Chaurasia, a passionate artist with a burning love for drawing, especially portraits. For the past 3 years, I've poured my heart and soul into this art form, honing my skills and refining my technique. Every stroke of my pencil is a testament to my dedication, and every finished portrait a window into the soul of my subject.Every stroke whispers a journey, every portrait a testament to the connection between art and the human spirit. Let me join you on this creative journey - explore my portfolio and see if I can weave your story onto canvas.
